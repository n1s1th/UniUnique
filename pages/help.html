Help & Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about UniUnique.

How do I list an item on the Marketplace?

Go to Marketplace → “List Your Item”, complete the form, and submit it.

Can I edit or delete my listing?

Yes. Use your dashboard to manage your listings.

Who can see my contact number?

Only logged-in users can see contact numbers for safety.

How do I report a suspicious listing?

Click “Report” on the item or email support@uniunique.com.

Do I need an account to view listings?

No. Viewing is public, but posting or contacting sellers requires login.

Is UniUnique officially affiliated with the university?

No. It's a student-run initiative created for the university community.
